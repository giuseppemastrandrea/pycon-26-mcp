# PyCon Italia 2026 — FAQ

## How is the conference structured?
- **May 27, 2026**: Open Learning Day, a day of workshops and hands-on sessions completely free and open to everyone (no conference ticket is necessary to participate in this event).
- **From May 27 to May 30**: Main conference (Talks, Workshops, and more). Buy tickets at https://pycon.it/tickets

## Where and when is the conference?
PyCon Italia will be held from May 27 to May 30, 2026 in Bologna (Italy), at the **Savoia Hotel Regency** (https://www.savoia.eu/en/index.html). Visit the https://pycon.it/where page for more details.

## What is the language of the conference?
The official language of the conference is English. Announcements, keynotes, opening/closing sessions, and more will be in English. This is because we are an international conference welcoming hundreds of individuals from around the world, and it is essential to ensure that everyone can participate and enjoy Python programming language with us!

If you speak Italian or prefer to attend a session in Italian, no worries! We aim to include at least one talk in Italian for every slot, so you will always have something interesting to follow!

If you have questions or issues, our staff will be more than happy to assist you! We speak both languages (and some speak even more!).

## Do you offer financial assistance?
Go to https://pycon.it/grants-info to find out more.

## Can I add co-speakers?
Yes! You can have as many co-speakers as you wish (in moderation!). We provide a free ticket for one speaker, while the first co-speaker will receive a 25% discount.

## Can I request a refund?
Check our refund policy page at https://pycon.it/refund-policies.

## Split Payment
To pay for your ticket via split payment, you need to select the 'Pay via bank transfer' option and then contact us at help@pycon.it.
